You can think about a lot of different things all the time. Think about too many things and this becomes a serious problem, especially for those who have ADD. If you have a problem focusing on which ideas to pursue, the sustainable thinker always wins.

The key is not to have the most ideas, but to follow through on the right ones. A sustainable approach means:

- Limiting the number of active projects
- Finishing what you start before moving on
- Building habits that support long-term productivity
- Taking breaks to avoid burnout

The tortoise beats the hare not because it's faster, but because it keeps moving forward consistently. The same applies to thinking and creating.
