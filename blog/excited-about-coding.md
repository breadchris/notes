I am really happy with the progress that I have been making with the site. I feel like I have really embraced the ethos of hacking and I find myself having an easier time letting go of what my past self thought was important.

The key has been focusing on shipping things rather than perfecting them. Every iteration brings new insights, and the momentum of constant improvement keeps the energy high.

When you're genuinely excited about coding, everything else falls into place. The late nights don't feel like work, the bugs become puzzles to solve, and each feature shipped is a small victory worth celebrating.
